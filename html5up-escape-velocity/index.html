Welcome

Luna Launderettes, founded in 1992, is a small family-run laundry service. We've been supplying families and students in Bristol with high-quality laundry and dry cleaning services for years. We take pleasure in providing dependable, friendly, and high-quality service.

Why Use Us?

Why Use Luna Launderettes?

Here's a list of features Luna Launderette provides.

Wash Time

A typical residential washing machine takes 50 minutes to finish washing; our washing machines take half that time.

Drying

Our launderettes' dryers have a large drum capacity, helping the material fibres relax while tumbling through the warm wet air, reducing the amount of ironing necessary when taken out of the dryer and folded neatly.

Load Capacity

Our laundries have larger washing machines than household machines. A conventional home machine takes about 4.5Kgs (10 lbs.) of dry washing, but our smallest machine takes 7Kgs (15 12 lbs.), and our most extensive appliance takes 28Kgs (62 lbs.) of dry washing (33lbs).

PayPoint Systems

Unlike most launderettes, our machines are all operated from a single point of payment. This provides benefits to our consumers that are not offered at many other laundries. £2 - £1 - 50p - 20p - 10p coins are accepted by the Central PayPoint system. There's no need to stress about how many coins you'll need.
